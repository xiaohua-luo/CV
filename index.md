Xiao-Hua Luo is a hematologist and immunologist at The First Affiliated Hospital of Chongqing Medical University, focusing on transplantation immunology, cellular therapy, and leukemia research.

## Research Interests

- **HSCT:** immune reconstitution, relapse, and post-transplant complications  
- **Advanced cellular therapies:** intrinsic resistance and long-term outcomes  
- **Acute myeloid leukemia:** novel therapeutic strategies and mechanisms
